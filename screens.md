# App Screens

This is a user-friendly list of screens and what each one is for.

1. App Shell - Main app container with bottom/rail navigation.
2. Home - Shows music feed, recommendations, and quick search entry.
3. Chip - Opens category/filter-based music results.
4. Browse - Shows details and songs for playlists, albums, or artists.
5. Search - Search songs, artists, albums, and playlists.
6. Player - Full now-playing screen with controls, queue, and lyrics.
7. Library - Main library hub for your saved content.
8. Favourites - Shows songs you have liked/saved.
9. History - Shows your recently played tracks.
10. Playlist Details - Shows songs inside a custom playlist.
11. Downloads - Main page for downloaded music.
12. Downloading - Shows active or queued downloads.
13. Download Playlist - Download songs from a selected playlist.
14. Settings - Main settings hub.
15. About - App info, version, links, and developer/community info.
16. Appearance - Theme, accent color, and visual preferences.
17. Backup and Storage - Backup, restore, and storage folder settings.
18. Player Settings - Playback options and player behavior settings.
19. Equalizer - Audio tuning and sound enhancement controls.
20. Privacy - Privacy-related preferences (history, personalization, etc.).
21. YouTube Music Settings - Region, language, quality, and visitor ID options.
